The Evolution of Computer Science

From early calculating machines to modern artificial intelligence, computer science has transformed our world.

1837 - Analytical Engine

Charles Babbage designed the first general-purpose computer, laying the foundation for modern computing.

1936 - Turing Machine

Alan Turing introduced the concept of a universal machine that could solve any computable problem.

1969 - ARPANET

The precursor to the internet was created, revolutionizing global communication and information sharing.

Today - AI Revolution

Machine learning and artificial intelligence are transforming industries and creating new possibilities.
